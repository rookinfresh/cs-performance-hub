Definitions & Scoring
Composite Score ▾

Equal-weight average of up to 4 pillars: AR velocity, call quality, retention SLA, and UFR pacing. Each normalized 0–1, scaled to 0–100.

Color Scale
● ≥ 80 On Track ● 50–79 Watch ● < 50 Critical
Pillar Weights

AI/SOA, Mid Market, Strategic: 4 pillars — 25% each
Phones + Platform: 3 pillars (no UFR) — 33% each
Pillars with no data are excluded dynamically.

Formula
(AR + Call + SLA + UFR) ÷ active_pillars × 100
AR / Day ▾

Account Reviews per active business day (L30). Counts closed SFDC ARs, Gong calls ≥15 min, and Podium calls ≥15 min. Deduplicated per org per day.

Call Quality ▾

AI-scored call quality (0–100) averaged across scored calls in L90. Powered by Cortex AI analyzing Gong and SFDC transcripts.

Color Scale
● ≥ 80 ● 70–79 ● < 70
4 Subscores

Value Creation — product recommendations, optimization, ROI
Relationship — listening, empathy, personalization, follow-through
Adaptiveness — reading the room, handling objections
Ownership — action items, timelines, proactive next steps

Retention SLA ▾

% of retention cases where outreach was made to the same org within 48 hours of case creation (L30). Self-created cases excluded — only externally-created cases count.

Color Scale
● ≥ 80% ● 50–79% ● < 50%
What Counts as Outreach

Closed SFDC AR, Gong call, or Podium call of any duration (including unanswered) by any CSM to the same org, on or after case creation.

UFR Coverage & Pacing ▾

% of renewal cohort orgs contacted. Color is based on pacing ratio (actual ÷ expected), not raw coverage %.

Pacing Color
● ≥ 100% On pace ● 70–100% ● < 70%

30% coverage can be green if only 25% was expected by now.

Outreach Window

3 months prior (1st) → 2 months prior (last day)
Example: Jun renewals = Mar 1 – Apr 30

What Counts

Closed SFDC AR, Gong ≥15 min, or Podium ≥15 min. M2M toggle available in filters.

Risk & Cancellation Intent ▾

AI-powered org-level churn risk from Cortex AI. Analyzes all CSM-customer interactions daily for risk signals and cancellation intent.

Risk Levels
● Low ● Elevated ● High ● Critical

Note: Risk has near-zero correlation with composite. A green CSM can have high-risk orgs.
