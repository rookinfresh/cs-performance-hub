Metric Definitions
Composite Score
( Normalized AR Score + Call Quality Score + Ret SLA Score + UFR Pacing Score ) ÷ active pillars × 100

Equal-weight average of active pillar scores (0–1 each, scaled to 0–100). AR is segment-normalized against a segment-specific ceiling. UFR uses a pacing model (not flat coverage). P+P segment is excluded from UFR pillar. Pillars are excluded when no data exists (e.g., no ret cases → SLA excluded). ≥ 65 green · ≥ 45 yellow · < 45 red.

AR Velocity — ARs / Day (Segment-Normalized)
AR Score = min( arsPerDay ÷ segment_ceiling, 1.0 )

Closed SFDC Account Review, Gong call ≥ 15 min, or Podium call ≥ 15 min — deduplicated per org per day. Score is normalized against a segment-specific ceiling so high-volume segments don't inflate composite.

Call Quality Score
Avg V3 AI score across scored calls in L90, expressed 0–100

V3 Cortex AI pipeline. Sub-scores: Value Creation · Relationship · Adaptiveness · Ownership. Source: V3_*_CALL_SCORING_PROCESSED — manual refresh ~weekly. CSMs without scored calls have this pillar excluded from composite. ≥ 80 green · ≥ 70 yellow · < 70 red.

Retention SLA
Retention tasks with outreach ÷ Total retention tasks (L30) × 100

fastball_Retention_Case tasks. Any closed SFDC AR, Gong call, or Podium call on/after task creation date counts. CSMs without ret cases have this pillar excluded. ≥ 80% green · 50–80% amber · < 50% red.

10-in-14 Compliance
10in14 tasks with outreach ÷ Total 10in14 tasks (L30) × 100

fastball_10in14_Churn_Risk tasks. Tracked as a separate indicator — no longer gates the composite score. Unattempted tasks are flagged with a warning icon in the CSM profile.

Renewal (UFR) Coverage — Pacing Model
Pacing Score = min( orgs_contacted ÷ expected_contacts_by_today, 1.0 ) Expected contacts = cohort_size × (biz_days_elapsed ÷ active_window_biz_days)

Non-Trane orgs renewing in the selected calendar month. P+P segment excluded from composite. The active outreach window runs from 120 days to 60 days before renewal (target: complete all contacts by 60 days out). Contacts made after the 60-day mark still count toward UFR coverage, but pacing is measured against the active window. Any closed SFDC AR, Gong call ≥ 15 min, or Podium call ≥ 15 min counts. Color coding: on/ahead of pace = green · 70–100% of pace = amber · < 70% of pace = red.

Interaction Intelligence — Risk Scores

Org-level churn risk scores powered by Podium's interaction intelligence model. Scores are bucketed into low · elevated · high · critical risk. The CSM profile panel shows a risk bar breakdown and flags cancellation intent signals detected in recent conversations. Risk data is sourced from the master export and may lag the live model by 24–48 hours.

Trend Data (Sparklines)

Daily snapshots of each CSM's composite score, AR velocity, call score, and retention SLA are captured in CS_PERF_HUB_SNAPSHOTS. The procedure snapshots metrics before each daily rebuild at 6 AM MST. Sparklines show the trailing 30-day trend.

Risk Intelligence

Risk data comes from BUILD.CUSTOMER_SUCCESS.CORE_INTERACTION_INTELLIGENCE_PROCESSED, a Cortex AI model (Claude Sonnet 4.6) that analyzes all CSM-customer interactions daily. It outputs: overall risk level (low/elevated/high/critical), sentiment trajectory, cancellation intent signals, and open ask tracking. Risk is attributed to CSMs based on org ownership.
