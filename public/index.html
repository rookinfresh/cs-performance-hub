Metric Definitions & Calculation Logic

Composite Score
( AR Velocity Score + Call Quality Score + Retention SLA Score ) ÷ 3 × 100

Equal-weight avg of three pillar scores (0–100). ≥ 67 green · ≥ 33 yellow · < 33 red. 10-in-14 gate overrides green → yellow if unattempted outreach exists.

AR Velocity — ARs / Day
Total AR Events (L30) ÷ Active Business Days (L30)

Closed SFDC Account Review, Gong call ≥ 15 min, or Podium call ≥ 15 min — deduplicated per org per day. Active days = weekdays where ≥ 3 CSMs logged any event (handles lag and holidays).

Call Quality Score
Avg V3 AI score across scored calls in L90, expressed 0–100

V3 Cortex AI pipeline across HVAC, Auto, MedSpa, Emerging. Trane uses V3 HVAC model. Sub-scores: Value Creation · Relationship · Adaptiveness · Ownership. Source: V3_*_CALL_SCORING_PROCESSED — manual refresh ~weekly.

Retention SLA
Retention tasks with outreach ÷ Total retention tasks (L30) × 100

fastball_Retention_Case tasks from BUILD.LIGHTHOUSE.CORE_TASKS. Any closed SFDC AR, Gong call, or Podium call on/after task creation date counts as outreach.

10-in-14 Compliance
10in14 tasks with outreach ÷ Total 10in14 tasks (L30) × 100

fastball_10in14_Churn_Risk tasks. Any unattempted task caps overall status at yellow regardless of composite score.

Renewal Coverage
Renewal orgs with outreach in 120d window ÷ Total cohort orgs × 100

Non-Trane orgs renewing next calendar month. Any closed SFDC AR, Gong call ≥ 15 min, or Podium call ≥ 15 min within 120 days pre-renewal counts.
